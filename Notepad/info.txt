         <h5 class="mb-3 text-center">Integrated Services Digital Network (ISDN)</h5>

         <p class="text-center">
             ISDN is a digital dial-up technology that provides faster internet access compared to traditional analog dial-up. It utilizes digital signaling over existing telephone lines to achieve speeds of up to 128 Kbps or higher. 
             ISDN connections require special ISDN modems and may involve dialing a specific phone number to establish the connection.
         </p>
